2025–2026 is the inflection point. YouTube is no longer just for creators. Brands that move now will own their category on the world's second-largest search engine.

01
The 2nd Biggest Search Engine
YouTube has 2.7 billion users and 3 billion monthly searches — second only to Google. Is your brand showing up when potential customers search for your top keywords? Google now surfaces Shorts in its main search results. Check the Shorts tab for your category. Are your videos there?
02
The Early Adopter Window
This is the 2000s moment for YouTube — when smart brands claimed their social handles early. Creators spent 10–20 years proving the platform works. Brands are just starting. Organic reach is still cheap. Our videos typically get 10× more views on YouTube than LinkedIn. The window will not stay open.
03
Authenticity in an AI World
In a world flooded with AI-generated content, authentic human video is your biggest differentiator. Video builds trust, shortens sales cycles, and creates real connection. Companies are now hiring dedicated content creators for YouTube. The shift has already begun.
