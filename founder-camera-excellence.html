Why This Exists

You're more capable than
your camera shows.

In a room, you're sharp. On camera, something shifts. This day is built to close that gap — for good.

01

"I look smaller on camera than I do in a room"

The energy drops. The presence disappears. On camera you feel like a shadow of yourself.

02

"My voice sounds flat when I play it back"

In a meeting, your voice carries weight. On camera, the warmth and authority are lost.

03

"I don't know where to look or what to do with my hands"

Eye contact, posture, gestures — the mechanics consume you instead of the message.

04

"I rush through everything and forget to pause"

When nervous, you speed up. The pauses that make you persuasive disappear entirely.

05

"Confident in the room — paralysed in front of the camera"

You know your material. But the camera triggers a different, smaller version of you.

Do I need prior experience with public speaking or video?
+
No prior experience needed. This works from wherever you're starting — whether you've never been on camera or you post regularly but want to raise your level significantly.
Will I walk away with edited videos?
+
No — and that's intentional. This program is entirely focused on skills, not content output. You will be filmed during exercises for coaching and diagnosis, but nothing is edited or packaged. For edited video content, the Weekend Accelerator is built for that.
How does the cohort work with just one coach?
+
The morning group sessions work naturally with 5–10 founders. The afternoon uses a hot-seat model — one founder is coached while the rest observe. Watching someone else get coached is one of the fastest ways to accelerate your own learning.
How is this different from the Weekend Accelerator?
+
The Weekend Accelerator (2 days, 7 coaches) focuses on producing 15–20 fully edited, publish-ready videos — skills development is built in, but the output is content. Founder Camera Excellence is the opposite: skills-only, no content output. Many founders do Camera Excellence first to build their presence, then come to the Weekend to record with that foundation in place.
Can I run this for my founding team?
+
Yes. This can be run as a company team program. Write to us at hello@100circles.com and we'll put together a format for your team's size and goals.
