Data Insights

27,624 views later, the data has opinions.

Nine of them - all uncomfortable in the best possible way.

🚀
YouTube is the Primary View Engine

YouTube drove 58.6% of all views and 66% of all watch hours. At ~540 views/video, it was 10× more efficient per video than LinkedIn.

⭐
One Video Carried Instagram

"This CEO is more famous…" generated 3,482 views - over 35% of Instagram's entire 30-day total alone. Without it, the platform average drops from 335 to ~216.

💼
LinkedIn Punches Above Its Weight

LinkedIn's 6.7% engagement rate is exceptional. Compared to Instagram at ~2.6%, LinkedIn's audience - while smaller - is far more action-oriented.

📺
YouTube Signals a Quality Audience

Avg CTR of 2.8% and 34.5% viewer retention. Bajaj Finance hit 61% retention + 6.3% CTR - showing what's possible when hook and topic perfectly align.

📉
LinkedIn Fatigue in the Back Half

LinkedIn views dropped from avg 71 (Days 1–10) to just 35 (Days 21–30) - a 51% decline. Daily posting likely triggered algorithm de-prioritisation.

🎯
CEO Stories = Viral Formula on Instagram

Top IG performers were CEO narratives and brand case studies - Red Bull, Bajaj Finance, Slack, Intercom. Human + brand consistently dominates Instagram.

⚡
AI & Tactical Content Wins YouTube

YouTube's top 5 by views were dominated by AI-related and platform-growth content. "How" and "Why X is big" structures performed best consistently.

📊
Viral Content Amplifies Watch Hours

Instagram generated 21.67 watch hours from just 9,859 views. The Red Bull video alone drove ~289 minutes. Viral content exponentially multiplies watch time.

🔄
Cross-Platform Consistency is Rare

Only 2–3 videos ranked top 5 on more than one platform. Persona + tactical hook is the proven cross-platform formula to replicate.
