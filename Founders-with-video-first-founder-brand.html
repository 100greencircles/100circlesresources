⚠ Significant Tier 3 Gap — 12 of 50 verified
This tier is structurally hard. Indian SaaS and tech founders at $50–100M revenue are typically public company executives, VC-backed growth companies with investor relations constraints, or bootstrapped founders who have deliberately maintained privacy. Out of 30+ founders researched at this tier, only 12 could be confirmed to have active, personal, face-to-camera video presence (not just company accounts or press interviews).
Recommendation: This tier reflects a genuine market gap in India's founder video ecosystem. Contrast with the US where founders like Aaron Levie (Box), Alexandr Wang (Scale AI) and Dheeraj Pandey (DevRev) are very active at this scale. India's equivalents are 18–24 months behind on this trend.
